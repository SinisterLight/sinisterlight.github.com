What I’m building

At Shovel, we are building ‘Stevens’ — a personal agent for the family that keeps up with the constant work of running a home: it’s local-first, with privacy and identity as structural principles (not verbal promises). We operate with ‘User Agency’ at the core, that comes from a decade of experience in distributed systems, identity, and the protocols. We build ‘tools’ — as they hand people more agency.

Stevens

A personal agent for the family. It watches the household quietly — bills, the kitchen, birthdays, the appointment you keep meaning to make — and acts on your behalf, across health, money, and the people you care about.

Meet Stevens →

The Creole Network

The substrate Stevens runs on — a user-controlled, local-first network that keeps a person’s data in their own hands. Everything else we’re building sits on top of it.
